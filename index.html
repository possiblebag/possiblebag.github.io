POSSIBLE is a space for the community to gather, express, and support itself. We are devoted to supporting and facilitating the creativity and wellness of our community. We currently offer a rehearsal space featuring a full backline of gear to support band/music practice. We welcome all musicians and performers seeking a clean and affordable place to practice their art. The space is also available for members of the community to gather and grow: art workshops, book clubs, shared interest meetups, etc. How to Book the Space 1) Email, text, or call us with your requested time and contact info. Cost is $20 per hour with a minimum booking of two hours. See "Rates" link for additional details. If you don't see "busy" in the calendar below, it means you can book it. 2) Once you receive confirmation, you can pay using Venmo, or pay cash at time of booking. 3) You are booked. A member of POSSIBLE will let you in the space at your booked time. Location and Practice Hours POSSIBLE is located in Boone at 3017 NC-105 See calendar below for available times.
